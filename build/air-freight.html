LAND FREIGHT SERVICES

Reliable and Efficient Land Transport Across the Kingdom and Beyond

JIT-Ex is your trusted partner for comprehensive land transportation services, ensuring your cargo reaches its destination safely and on time. With our extensive network, advanced fleet, and deep industry expertise, we provide unparalleled land freight solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of businesses across Saudi Arabia, the GCC, and beyond.

WHY CHOOSE JIT-EX FOR LAND FREIGHT?

With Transport Certification No. 050111030900 and Commercial Registration No. 2050074725, First Step Establishment for Transportation Services (JIT-Ex) is a leading force in land transportation across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and neighboring regions. Our services extend to over 100 destinations, including the GCC countries, Yemen, Syria, Jordan, Lebanon, and Egypt, ensuring that no matter where your goods need to go, JIT-Ex can get them there efficiently and reliably.

Vast Reach Across Key Markets

Our land freight services cover the entire Kingdom and major trade routes across the GCC and neighboring countries. This extensive reach allows us to serve a diverse range of industries and clients, ensuring that your shipments are delivered to even the most remote locations.

Our commitment to Just In Time (JIT) service means we prioritize speed without compromising on reliability. We work with leading airlines and logistics partners to ensure your cargo is airborne as soon as possible and delivered right on schedule.

Impressive Daily Capacity

JIT-Ex manages over 200 truck shipments per day, making us one of the most active land transport providers in the region. Our high daily shipment volume is a testament to the trust our flagship clients place in us and our ability to handle large-scale logistics operations with precision and reliability.

Comprehensive Fleet for Diverse Needs

Our advanced fleet includes a variety of vehicles such as reefers, flatbeds, dynas, box trailers, tankers, and container trucks, capable of handling all types of cargo, including temperature-sensitive goods, oversized shipments, and dangerous goods (DG and non-DG). Whether you need to transport perishable items, heavy machinery, or hazardous materials, JIT-Ex has the right equipment to get the job done.

Direct Control with No Third Parties

JIT-Ex maintains direct control over all aspects of our land freight services, with no reliance on third-party operators. This ensures consistent service quality, faster response times, and full accountability at every stage of the transport process. Our clients benefit from our in-house expertise, ensuring that their cargo is handled with the utmost care and efficiency.

Custom Solutions for Every Client

We understand that each client has unique logistics requirements, which is why we offer customized land freight solutions. Whether you need regular deliveries, one-time shipments, or specialized transport for complex cargo, JIT-Ex tailors our services to meet your specific needs.
